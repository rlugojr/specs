About

The purpose of w3viewer is to provide a good overview of the web standards, including standards about HTML, CSS, and the JS APIs.

Usage

The blue area on the left is the panel. Notice the tabbed navigation on the top of the panel. Each tab represents one catergory. Each category contains a list of entries sorted into groups. Hover over entries to display their corresponding info boxes.

Click on an entry to open the default document for that entry - this is usually the latest W3C TR. Notice how each info box contains one or more resources at the bottom. To open these additional resources, press the corresponding key command.

Once a resource is opened, click on the entry in the panel again to close it. You will notice a red "X" button at the right end of the entry - you don't need to target that button, just click anywhere on the entry.
